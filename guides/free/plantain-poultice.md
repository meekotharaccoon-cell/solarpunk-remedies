## Overview

**Plants used:** broadleaf plantain (Plantago major), narrowleaf plantain (Plantago lanceolata)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** spring through fall (fresh), year-round (dried leaves reconstituted)

## What it helps with

- Bug bites
- Bee stings
- Minor cuts
- Splinter drawing
- Rash relief

## Basic preparation

1. Identify plantain leaves in your yard (broad ribbed leaves growing in a rosette, not the banana-like fruit).
2. Harvest several fresh, clean leaves -- avoid areas treated with pesticides.
3. Wash leaves thoroughly under running water.
