## Overview

**Plants used:** lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** mid-summer (harvest), year-round (stored oil)

## What it helps with

- Headache relief
- Stress reduction
- Sleep improvement
- Minor burn care
- Muscle relaxation

## Basic preparation

1. Harvest lavender stems when about half the flower buds have opened.
2. Bundle and hang upside down to dry for 1-2 weeks in a dark, well-ventilated space.
3. Strip dried flowers from stems and lightly crush them.
