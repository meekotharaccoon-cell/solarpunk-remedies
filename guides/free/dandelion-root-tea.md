## Overview

**Plants used:** dandelion (Taraxacum officinale)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** early spring or late fall (harvest), year-round (dried root)

## What it helps with

- Liver support
- Digestive aid
- Mild diuretic
- Nutrient-rich tonic

## Basic preparation

1. Dig dandelion roots in early spring or late fall when energy is stored in the root.
2. Wash roots thoroughly, scrubbing off all soil.
3. Chop roots into small pieces (about 1/4 inch).
