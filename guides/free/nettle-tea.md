## Basic preparation

1. Wear thick gloves to harvest young nettle tops in early spring (top 4-6 inches).
2. Rinse thoroughly -- cooking or drying neutralizes the sting.
3. To dry: spread on screens in a warm, ventilated area for 1-2 weeks.
