## Overview

**Plants used:** calendula (Calendula officinalis)
**Difficulty:** intermediate
**Best season:** summer (harvest), year-round (stored salve)

## What it helps with

- Minor wound healing
- Dry skin
- Rash relief
- Diaper rash
- Chapped lips

## Basic preparation

1. Harvest calendula flowers at midday when fully open and dry.
2. Dry flowers completely on screens for 1-2 weeks (moisture causes mold).
3. Fill a jar halfway with dried calendula petals.
