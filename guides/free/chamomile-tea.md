## Overview

**Plants used:** German chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** summer (harvest), year-round (dried)

## What it helps with

- Sleep aid
- Anxiety relief
- Digestive calming
- Mild pain relief

## Basic preparation

1. Harvest chamomile flowers when petals are fully open and beginning to fold back.
2. Dry flowers on a screen in a warm, well-ventilated area for 1-2 weeks.
3. Use 1 tablespoon dried flowers (or 2 tablespoons fresh) per cup.
