## Overview

**Plants used:** ginger (Zingiber officinale)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** year-round (ginger root available from stores)

## What it helps with

- Muscle pain relief
- Joint stiffness
- Menstrual cramp relief
- Improving local circulation

## Basic preparation

1. Grate 4-5 tablespoons of fresh ginger root (do not peel if organic).
2. Wrap grated ginger in cheesecloth and tie into a bundle.
3. Bring 1 quart of water to just below boiling (do not let it boil).
