**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** year-round (turmeric powder readily available)

## What it helps with

- Inflammation reduction
- Joint pain
- Antioxidant support

## Basic preparation

1. Combine 1/2 cup turmeric powder with 1 cup water in a saucepan.
2. Add 1/3 cup coconut oil or olive oil (fat helps absorption of curcumin).
3. Add 1-1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper (piperine increases curcumin absorption by 2000%).

## Safety notes

Turmeric stains everything yellow -- protect clothing and surfaces. May interact with blood thinners, diabetes medications, and stomach acid reducers. High doses may cause digestive upset. Consult a healthcare provider if you have gallbladder issues or are pregnant.
