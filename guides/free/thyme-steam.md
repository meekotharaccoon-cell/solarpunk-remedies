## Overview

**Plants used:** thyme (Thymus vulgaris)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** summer (harvest fresh), year-round (dried)

## What it helps with

- Nasal congestion
- Sinus relief
- Chest congestion
- Bronchitis support

## Basic preparation

1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.
2. Remove from heat and add a large handful of fresh thyme (or 3 tablespoons dried).
3. Let steep for 2-3 minutes.

## Safety notes

Keep face at least 12 inches from the water to prevent burns. Not suitable for young children due to burn risk. Discontinue if breathing becomes difficult. People with asthma should use with caution as steam may trigger spasms.
