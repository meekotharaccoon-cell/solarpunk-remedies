## Basic preparation

1. Harvest lemon balm leaves before the plant flowers for best flavor and potency.
2. Use 2 tablespoons fresh leaves (or 1 tablespoon dried) per cup.
3. Tear or bruise fresh leaves to release aromatic oils.
