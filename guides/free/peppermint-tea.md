## Overview

**Plants used:** peppermint (Mentha x piperita)
**Difficulty:** beginner
**Best season:** summer (harvest), year-round (dried)

## What it helps with

- Nausea relief
- Digestive aid
- Bloating reduction
- Headache relief
- Breath freshener

## Basic preparation

1. Harvest peppermint leaves just before the plant flowers for peak oil content.
2. Rinse leaves and pat dry, or dry on screens for 1-2 weeks for storage.
3. Use 5-10 fresh leaves (or 1 tablespoon dried) per cup.
