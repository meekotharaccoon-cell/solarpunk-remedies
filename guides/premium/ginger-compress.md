## Plants & Identification

- **ginger (Zingiber officinale)**

Always positively identify plants before harvesting. Use a reliable field guide or consult an experienced forager. When wildcrafting, harvest sustainably — never take more than 1/3 of a plant stand.

## Full Preparation Steps

**Step 1.** Grate 4-5 tablespoons of fresh ginger root (do not peel if organic).

**Step 2.** Wrap grated ginger in cheesecloth and tie into a bundle.

**Step 3.** Bring 1 quart of water to just below boiling (do not let it boil).

**Step 4.** Turn off heat and squeeze the ginger bundle into the hot water, letting it steep.

**Step 5.** Dip a clean washcloth into the ginger water (use tongs -- it will be very hot).

**Step 6.** Wring out excess liquid carefully.

**Step 7.** Apply the warm cloth to the affected area (sore muscles, stiff joints).

**Step 8.** Cover with a dry towel to retain heat. Replace when cloth cools. Repeat 3-4 times per session.
